What's Fuiibi ???

For the short story, Fuiibi means in Latin "I have been there"

Fuiibi is an application that allow attendees of a specific event to share his photos with the other attendees of the event.

You can open new event and invite your friends to share their photos linked to the event.

Sharing photos become much easier and every thing is on the cloud, no more storage issue on your mobile, and in a second you can find your and your friends pictures of a specific event.

And now just enjoy!
